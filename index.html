Renewable Energy Generation from Wastewater and Rainwater Using a 3D-Printed Turbine

Collaborated on a mini hydro-power prototype that channels wastewater and rainwater through a nozzle to spin a 3D-printed Pelton wheel connected to a 6V DC motor. Built and demonstrated the setup with a 2-liter elevated water tank, producing enough electricity to light an LED.
